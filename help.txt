course [course] (sortCriteria):
    Searches data for course named [course].
    Displays teachers teaching course and their ratings.
    sortCriteria are:
    - "d" | "diff" | "difficulty"
    - "r" | "rating"
    - "c" | "count"
    - "t" | "retake"
help:
    Displays this help message. Very useful!
quit:
    Terminates the program and exits.
teacher [teacher]:
    Searches data for teacher named [teacher],
    Displays their ratings and courses taught.
